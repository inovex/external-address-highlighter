Name:

External Address Highlighter (Google Inbox)


Shortname:

?


Description:

Highlights mail-recipients with external addresses.


Detailed Description:

While composing an email, this extension gives visual feedback
when a recipient has an "external" email-address.

External typically means "outside of the company".
This can be configured with a regular expression.

It works with Google Inbox.
